How it works

Three steps. None of them are yours to figure out.

Most patients in Ireland spend weeks calling insurers, GPs and consultants' secretaries trying to piece it together. Coverly does it once, properly.

  1. 01

    We read your cover

    Tell us your insurer and plan name — or upload your policy document. We extract exactly what's covered, what the excess is, and which specialists settle directly.

    About 2 minutes
  2. 02

    We match an approved specialist

    We surface in-network consultants for your condition, ranked by availability, location and your out-of-pocket cost. Every figure shown — excess, settlement status, fee — comes directly from your plan documents. You choose who to book with.

    Quickly handled, no chasing required
  3. 03

    We handle the booking

    Once you choose a specialist, we coordinate the referral with your GP, confirm the appointment with the consultant's rooms, and submit pre-authorisation to your insurer on your behalf. You receive a single confirmation — nothing is done without your approval.

    One confirmation, every detail handled

Common questions

Straight answers before you start.

Do I need a referral letter to begin?+

No. You can start with just your insurer and plan name. If a referral is needed for your specialist, we coordinate it with your GP as part of the booking.

Which insurers do you work with?+

Vhi, Laya Healthcare, Irish Life Health and HSF Health — the four insurers covering essentially the entire Irish private health market. We keep plan structures current so the cover we show you matches what your insurer will actually settle.

Are you affiliated with my insurer?+

No. Coverly is fully independent of all insurers. We work for the patient — which is why we can tell you the real out-of-pocket cost, not just the headline.

Are you regulated? Do you give insurance advice?+

Coverly is a healthcare navigation service, not an insurance intermediary. We do not sell, compare or advise on insurance products, and we do not handle claims. What we do is read the policy you already hold and tell you — in plain language — what it says: your excess, your covered specialists, your direct-settlement options. We present that information so you can make an informed decision. We never tell you what to claim or whether to claim.

What about consultants who aren't in-network?+

We show you in-network consultants first because that's almost always what patients want. If a specific consultant matters to you, we tell you exactly what your plan will and won't reimburse so there are no surprises.

How quickly will I have an appointment?+

The match is usually within an hour. The appointment itself depends on the consultant's availability — we sort options by soonest available so you can choose.
